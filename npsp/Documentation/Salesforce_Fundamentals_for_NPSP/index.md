## Get Started: Salesforce Fundamentals for Nonprofits

For nonprofits with limited resources, figuring out how to get started with the powerful Salesforce

platform can feel overwhelming. But don't worry! We're here to help.

### Fundamentals for Nonprofits

There are so many ways to learn about Salesforce. How you approach it depends on your learning style

and preferences. Want to learn online on your own time? Try Trailhead. Want to read through detailed

documentation, get one-on-one assistance, or talk to your peers? We've got you covered on all fronts!

We have an amazing collection of resources below designed to help you get started & be successful

with Salesforce.

### Get One-on-One Assistance

Know who to reach out to for all your questions about Salesforce and Nonprofit Success Pack.

**Account Executive**  If you have questions about your Salesforce product, or are interested in other

Salesforce products, you can contact your Account Executive. Log into your org to find your Account

Executive.

**Consulting Partner**  If you know you need major implementation support, we highly recommend hiring a

consulting partner. Most partners include training hours in their scope of work, in which they teach you

how to manage your Salesforce org on your own. Check out the Salesforce.org website to Find a Certified

**Consulting Partner** .

**Ask an Expert**  This program lets customers ask a Salesforce employee product adoption, best-practice

and how-to questions live. Find upcoming Ask An Expert sessions on the Customer Success Event

Calendar.

**Technical Support**  If you have a specific question about your Salesforce org, you can see How to Create a

Case on Salesforce Help. Every Salesforce.org customer has access to a Standard Success Plan which

includes access to Standard Support.

**Premier Success**  If you would like more than just standard support, and want access to more 1:1

resources, you may be interested in purchasing a Premier Success plan. For more information about

pricing for Premier Success, please reach out to us via the Contact Me form.

**Pro Bono**  Salesforce.org's Pro Bono Program matches nonprofits with Salesforce professionals to help

them with specific Salesforce challenges. Check out our website to Apply for Pro Bono Services.
